About Us

HarvestHub aims to democratize knowledge for all African farmers by giving them unrestricted access to agricultural information. By exposing local farmers to our resources like our Smart System and E-commerce marketplace, we can boost the quality of their products and increase their incentives.

Through the use of Web5 technologies, we will ensure added security to our platform hereby protecting our users from illegitimate transactions.
